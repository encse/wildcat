Inverted Shower

You pretty much do what you see here. It's easier if you grab the balls from the top, but you'll block your audience's view of the ball. I find this trick very difficult. I tend to bump the ball with my wrist as I'm carring across the top. If you like the look of this trick, but find it too difficult, try the Pendulum instead.
