Reverse Cascade

Before learning the Reverse Cascade, learn Over the Top, Tennis, and the Half Shower. The Reverse Cascade is over the top done on every throw from both hands. When throwing over the top, throw shorter throws. Instead of throwing to the opposite side of your body, throw to the center of your body.
